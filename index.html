    Step by Step instruction while preparing tea.

  1. First take a saucepan and one cup of water and boil it.
  2. Once the water is boiling add 2 teaspoon of sugar, add 3-4 crushed cardamom.
  3. Then add tealeaves and let it boil for a minute.
  4. Then add a cup of milk and bring it to boil.
  5. Now your delicious cup of tea is ready. Have it with any snacks of your choice.
